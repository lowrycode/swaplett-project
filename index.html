Aim of the Game

Swap the letters into the correct positions in the words within the specified number of swaps.

Words are either horizontal or vertical within the grid.

Before The Game

Choose word length

This determines how many letters are in the word and therefore the shape of the grid

Choose difficulty level

Choose from one of the options below:

  • EASY: can be solved within 6 swaps
  • MEDIUM: can be solved within 8 swaps
  • HARD: can be solved within 10 swaps
